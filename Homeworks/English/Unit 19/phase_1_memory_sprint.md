# Phase 1: Memory Sprint

This phase tests what you remember from the previous unit, *Magazines and Books*. Let's see how much you remember! You have 2 minutes. Tap the correct answers.

---

### Item 1
**Which sentence is grammatically correct for making a suggestion?**
A) Why not we to write a review?
B) Shall we including some photos?
C) **Let's go to the library after school.** ✓
D) Why don't we watching a film?

**Feedback for wrong answers:** Always use the base verb (without 'to' or '-ing') after "Let's", "Shall we", "Why not", and "Why don't we". UZ: Taklif bildirayotganda fe'lning asosiy shaklidan foydalanishingiz shart ("to" yoki "-ing" qo'shimchalarisiz).
`[Bloom: L2 | PISA: L1]`

---

### Item 2
**Choose the correct word to connect these ideas: "I didn't have time to read the book, ____ I watched the film instead."**
A) as
B) because
C) **so** ✓
D) when

**Feedback for wrong answers:** We use "so" to show the result of a situation. "As" and "because" are used to show a reason. UZ: Natijani ko'rsatish uchun "so" (shuning uchun) bog'lovchisidan foydalanamiz.
`[Bloom: L2 | PISA: L1]`

---

### Item 3
**What do we call a written opinion about a book, film, or TV programme?**
A) interview
B) **review** ✓
C) headline
D) advertisement

**Feedback for wrong answers:** A "review" tells you what a writer thinks about a book or film. An interview is a conversation, and a headline is a title. UZ: "Review" bu biror kitob yoki film haqidagi yozma fikr (taqriz).
`[Bloom: L1 | PISA: L1]`

---

### Item 4
**When we make a suggestion using "Why don't we..." or "Shall we...", we must put a full stop (.) at the end of the sentence.**
A) True
B) **False** ✓

**Feedback for wrong answers:** "Why don't we..." and "Shall we..." are questions, so they must always end with a question mark (?). UZ: "Why don't we" va "Shall we" so'roq gaplar hisoblanadi, shuning uchun ular doimo so'roq belgisi (?) bilan tugashi shart.
`[Bloom: L1 | PISA: L1]`
