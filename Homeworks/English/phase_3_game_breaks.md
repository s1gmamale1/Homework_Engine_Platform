# Phase 3: Game Breaks

Welcome to the Game Breaks! Now it is time to practice the new words and rules you have learned about school and trips. We have 3 fast games for you.

## Game 1: Adaptive Quiz
This game tests your grammar and translation skills. It starts easy and gets harder. Choose or write the correct answer.

**Item 1 (Easy):** Which sentence means "Siz buni bajarishingiz shart"?
A) You don't have to do it.
B) **You have to do it.** ✓
C) You haven't to do it.
`[Bloom: L1 | PISA: L1]`

**Item 2 (Easy):** Fill in the blank: "Every morning, I ________ tidy my bedroom."
A) **have to** ✓
B) has to
C) don't have to
`[Bloom: L2 | PISA: L1]`

**Item 3 (Medium):** How do you say "Men imtihondan o'tishim shart emas" in English?
A) I have to pass the test.
B) I haven't to pass the test.
C) **I don't have to pass a test.** ✓
`[Bloom: L2 | PISA: L2]`

**Item 4 (Medium):** Choose the correct question form: "________ you have to wear a uniform at your school?"
A) Are
B) **Do** ✓
C) Have
`[Bloom: L2 | PISA: L1]`

**Item 5 (Hard):** Write the missing words to complete the rule: "My dad works at home today, so he ________ (not/wear) a suit."
**Answer:** doesn't have to wear
`[Bloom: L3 | PISA: L2]`

**Item 6 (Hard):** Translate this sentence into English using the target grammar: "Biz muzeyga borishimiz shart emas."
**Answer:** We don't have to go round a museum. (or: We don't have to visit a museum.)
`[Bloom: L3 | PISA: L2]`
