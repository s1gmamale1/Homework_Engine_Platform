Mode: HARD
Level: B1

**Front:** have to
**Back:** Obligation — you have no choice. Misol: "Everyone **has to** be at school at eight o'clock." UZ: bajarish shart.

**Front:** don't have to
**Back:** No obligation — you have a choice. Misol: "You **don't have to** bring anything to eat." UZ: bajarish shart emas.

**Front:** take notes
**Back:** Collocation — to write down information. Misol: "I **took lots of notes** in my last lesson." UZ: qayd qilib bormoq.

**Front:** do a project
**Back:** Collocation — never "make a project". Misol: "I'm **doing a history project** at the moment." UZ: loyiha ustida ishlamoq.

**Front:** pass a test ≠ fail a test
**Back:** Pass = success, Fail = no success. Misol: "I usually **pass every test**, but I **failed that one**." UZ: imtihondan o'tmoq ≠ yiqilmoq.

**Front:** get marks
**Back:** Collocation — points or grades in an exam. Misol: "What **mark did you get** in your science exam?" UZ: baho olmoq.

**Front:** study from a textbook
**Back:** Collocation — to learn using a book. Misol: "Students **study at home from a textbook**." UZ: darslikdan o'qimoq.

**Front:** give a lesson
**Back:** Collocation — what a teacher does. Misol: "The teacher stands at the front of the room and **gives a lesson**." UZ: dars o'tmoq.

**Front:** do homework
**Back:** Collocation — never "make homework". Misol: "Then they go home and **do their homework**." UZ: uy vazifasini bajarmoq.

**Front:** nature walk
**Back:** A walk in the countryside to see plants and animals. Misol: "What to pack for **nature walks**: trainers." UZ: tabiat qo'ynida sayr.
