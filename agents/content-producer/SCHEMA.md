## Inline Tags — MANDATORY on Every Question

Every question, checkpoint, and game item MUST carry a tag:

```
[UZ-SUBJ-GRADE-TOPIC-SEQ] | Bloom: level | PISA: Level N     — Phase 1
[Bloom: L1-L2 | PISA: Level 1-2]                              — Phase 2 checkpoints
[Bloom: L1-L3 | PISA: Level 1-2]                              — Phase 3 game items
[Bloom: L3-L4 | PISA: Level 2-3]                              — Phase 4 questions
[UZ-SUBJ-GRADE-TOPIC-SEQ] | Bloom: level | PISA LN | skill    — Phase 6 boss Qs
```

A question without a tag is INCOMPLETE.

---

## Pronoun Policy

All Uzbek content uses **"Siz"** (formal). Never "sen". Russian: **"Вы"**, never "ты".

---

## Phase 1 Format Rules

**3 formats ONLY:** Multiple Choice, True/False, Yes/No/Not Given.
**MUST mix** at least 2 of 3 formats per sprint.
**BANNED:** Fill-in-blank, gap-fill, speed match, drag-and-drop, typing, open-ended.
Tap-only interaction.

---

## Phase 2 Story Rules

**ONE continuous story.** No segments, no labels, no "Segment 1:", no "Muammo:", no "Kurash:".
Arc beats (Problem→Struggle→Discovery→Solution) are invisible ingredients.
Checkpoints come AFTER the story.
