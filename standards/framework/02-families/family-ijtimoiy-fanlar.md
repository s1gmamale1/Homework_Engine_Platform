# Ijtimoiy Fanlar (Social Sciences / History)

## Purpose

Understanding human societies ‚Äî past events, political systems, legal frameworks, economic principles. Analytical thinking built through historical evidence and causal reasoning, not date memorization.

## Learning outcomes

- Understand historical events in their causal context (not just dates)
- Analyze primary sources and evaluate reliability
- Build causal chains ‚Äî "why did X happen" drilled multiple levels deep
- Compare periods, figures, and movements across time
- Connect historical knowledge to current events and civic responsibility

## Teaching methodology

- **Two-layer explanation:** academic analysis + simplified narrative
- **Narrative IS the content:** History is stories. Preview panels are the primary teaching vehicle ‚Äî events taught as engaging narratives, not fact lists
- **Causal chain building:** "Why did X happen?" chains, same depth structure as Why Chain game
- **Source analysis:** primary documents, quotes, artifacts ‚Äî students evaluate evidence rather than accept claims
- **Multiple perspectives:** same event from different viewpoints; history is interpreted, not fixed
- **National Pride (Milliylik) integration:** Uzbekistan history woven naturally into World History context ‚Äî contextual, not forced
- **Panel 4 (Origin) SKIPPED** ‚Äî the Preview narrative IS the origin story for history
