Notebook Capture

Universal K-11 Mechanic: Embodied Cognition & Vision-AI Hybrid

Scope: Grades 1-11 Tier: 3 (Multimodal AI) Retention: +25-40%

1. Universal Strategic Mission

The Notebook Capture is the foundational bridge between the digital platform and the physical classroom. By moving students from "tapping" to "handwriting," NETS triggers deep embodied cognition. This mechanic is utilized across all subjects where spatial reasoning, diagramming, or mathematical derivation is required.

The "Freeze" Protocol: To maximize focus across all grade levels, the session timer is paused during the "Drawing Phase." The system prioritizes task quality over speed.

2. General Rules & Player Journey

  • 1
    Physical Prompting
    Student receives a grade-appropriate task (e.g. sketching a cell for G5 or deriving a complex integral for G11).
  • 2
    Manual Execution
    The student works in their physical notebook. Digital pacer is paused.
  • 3
    Verified Capture
    The app uses real-time edge detection and focus validation. Capture is only permitted when the "Legibility Score" is high.

3. Multi-Level Scaling (L1 - L4)

Level Cognitive Mode K-11 Implementation Example
L1 Foundation Copy and label specific textbook diagrams (Primary focus).
L2 Visualization Create a Radiant Map (Mind Map) of chapter concepts.
L3 Application Full mathematical derivation or scientific schematic (Secondary focus).
L4 Innovation Original schematic design or complex proof construction (High-school focus).

4. Technical Integration

Validation Parameters

  • Laplacian Variance: Sharpness check.
  • Brightness Check: Contrast for OCR.
  • Edge detection: Perspective correction.
  • Privacy: Local blurring of faces/biometrics.

AI Feedback Strategy

The AI persona acts as a Socratic Mentor. It identifies structural errors in the student's handwriting but never provides the solution directly, asking questions to prompt self-correction.
