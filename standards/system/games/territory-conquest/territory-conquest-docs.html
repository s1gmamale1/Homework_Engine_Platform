1. Multi-Player Battle Royale

The field of battle expands to accommodate up to 6 total entities (Student + up to 5 AIs). Map scale increases dynamically (e.g., 10x10 for 4 players, 12x12 for 6 players).

  • Free for All (FFA): Last player standing. Every AI competes against the student and each other.
  • One for All (Premium Only): The "Horde Mode." All 5 AIs team up against the student. Survival requires expert use of Buzan Buffs.
  • The 10% Surge: Every game has a 10% chance of a "Reinforcement Event" where a new AI enemy joins the fray mid-game.

🧠 Buzan Integration: Radiant Retrieval

The game implements Tony Buzan's Mental Literacy principles to enhance memory and recall.

  • Radiant Maps: The territory grid is visually connected by "Mental Branches." Capturing a branch root grants the student the "Fog-Lifting" view of all sub-branches.
  • Color Hooks: Each territory type (Resource, Trap, Base) uses high-contrast, specific colors to trigger **Synesthesia** and easier recall during "Re-Conquest" attempts.
  • The Memory Palace (Special Tile): Capturing a "Palace Tile" requires the student to recall a sequence of 3-5 facts. Success grants a permanent +1 AP for the entire game.

5. Victory Conditions & Domination

A. Strategic Victory (Turn 12)

If the game reaches Turn 12 without a total knockout:

  • The player with the **highest territory count** is declared the winner.
  • In Premium matches, the rank is determined by the total area of influence across all 6 players.

B. Total Domination (The Knockout)

If a student captures the **Main Territory (Capital)** of an AI player, that AI is eliminated instantly, and all its territories turn neutral (gray).

  • The "Warlord" Bonus: Destroying ALL AI main territories before Turn 12 triggers an immediate victory.
  • Extra Rewards: Total Domination grants **Special Bonuses** (to be defined in Layer 4: Metagame) such as Hero Relics or Building Blueprints.
