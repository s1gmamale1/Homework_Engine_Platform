01. What Is This Family

"Ijtimoiy" translates to social or societal. This family is defined by its focus on human systems—institutions, events, laws, and economies.

Textbook Refinement Mandate

This family strictly refines the Uzbek National Textbooks. No outside teaching or international syllabi (like IELTS) are permitted. All content must activate the mandated curriculum.

03. Content Filter (Anchors)

Content producers must prioritize these cognitive anchors for Ijtimoiy Fanlari:

Perspective BEFORE Fact

Understand why an actor made a choice before memorizing the date.

Source-to-Reasoning Flow

Every claim must be anchored to a primary or secondary source.

Discovery Narrative

Transform textbook facts into a human story (Default Mode).

Case-Based Assessment

Evaluate understanding through "What would you do?" scenarios.

National-Global Dialectic

Strictly maintain the 55/45 origin balance (Uzbekistan vs. World references).

04. Best Methods & Games

Timeline Sort (Tarixiy Kenglik)

Primary Mechanic · Logic: Causal Sequence

Arranging events, laws, or procedures in chronological order.

Good Example "Arrange the stages of the Silk Road's development in Samarkand."

Source Comparison (Manbalar To'qnashuvi)

Primary Mechanic · Logic: Bias Detection

Presenting conflicting accounts of the same event to build critical thinking.

Good Example "Compare a Roman historian's account with a Sogdian merchant's perspective."

Why Chain (Sabablar Zanjiri)

Primary Mechanic · Logic: Multi-level Causality

Building cause-and-effect trees (Level 1 → Level 2 → Level 3).

Bad Example "What is the definition of inflation?" (Static Recall)
