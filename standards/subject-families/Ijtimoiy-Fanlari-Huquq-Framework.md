## 1. SUBJECT IDENTITY: THE PILLAR OF JUSTICE

**Huquq (Law)** in the NETS ecosystem is the study of **principles, rights, and responsibilities** that govern society. It focuses on the ability to interpret legal texts and apply them to real-world disputes, fostering a culture of justice and civic duty.

### 1.1 Core Pillars of Huquq
- **Constitutional Literacy:** Deep understanding of the 2023 Uzbekistan Constitution as the supreme law.
- **Legal Reasoning:** Applying a general article or rule to a specific case (Syllogistic logic).
- **Rights & Duties:** Balancing personal liberties with societal responsibilities.
- **Institutional Understanding:** How the court system and government branches function.

---

## 2. SUBJECT-SPECIFIC COGNITIVE DEMANDS

Following the Ijtimoiy Fanlari profile, Huquq prioritizes:
1. **Case Reasoning:** "Given these facts, which law was broken?"
2. **Textual Interpretation:** Analyzing the language of legal articles for specific meaning.
3. **Principled Decision Making:** Defending a verdict based on evidence and law.
4. **Normative Synthesis:** Understanding how laws evolve with society.

## 3. GAME MECHANICS & PEDAGOGY (HUQUQ TUNED)

### 3.1 Primary Mechanics
- **Source Comparison (Manbalar To'qnashuvi):** Comparing different versions of a law or conflicting testimonies in a simulated trial.
- **Tile Match (Article Assembly):** Matching specific legal terms to their definitions or grouping articles by branch (Civil, Criminal, Labor).
- **Escape Room:** Solving "Legal Riddles" (e.g., "Find the missing article to unlock the courtroom door").
- **Why Chain:** Mapping the consequences of a legal violation through society (e.g., Crime → Investigation → Trial → Verdict).

### 3.2 Mnemonic Systems (Buzan Integration)
- **Radiant Summary (Mind Map):** Creating a multi-colored map of the **2023 Constitution** branches.
- **Memory Palace:** Using the **Ark Fortress** in Bukhara to "store" the key rights of citizens in different chambers.
- **SMASHIN' SCOPE:** Creating vivid mental images for complex legal terminology (e.g., *Jurisprudensiya*).

## 4. NATIONAL PRIDE INJECTIONS (55/45 BALANCE)

Huquq anchors the student's civic identity.

- **National Focus (55%):** Deep dives into the **New Uzbekistan Constitution**, Amir Temur's "Tuzuklar" as a legal ancestor, and the role of the Mahalla in civic life.
- **Global Focus (45%):** Comparing national laws with international standards (e.g., UDHR, UN Charter, Child Rights Convention).
- **Wise Role:** `Bosh Qonunshunos` (Chief Legal Counsel).
- **Example Injection:** "Amir Temur 'Kuch adolatdadir' degan tamoyilni davlat boshqaruvining asosi deb bilgan. Bugungi kunda ushbu tamoyil Konstitutsiyamizda qanday o'z aksini topgan?"

## 5. BOSS FORMAT: THE JUDICIAL ANALYST

- **Format:** **Legal Case Reasoning**.
- **The Task:** "A citizen claims their right to privacy was violated by a local business. Based on Chapter VII of the Constitution, evaluate this claim and provide a 3-point justification for your verdict."
- **Evaluation:** Tier 2 LLM checks for **article reference**, **logical application**, and **justice-oriented tone**.
- **No MCQs:** All G8-11 Law sessions culminate in a written legal conclusion.
