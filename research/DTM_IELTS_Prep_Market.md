# Uzbekistan Prep Market: DTM & IELTS Analysis

This document maps the competitive landscape for university entrance (DTM/BBA) and language (IELTS) preparation in Uzbekistan.

## 1. DTM Prep Market (Bilimni Baholash Agentligi)

The DTM exam consists of a Mandatory Block (3 subjects) and a Specialty Block (2 subjects).

### Major Preparation Centers
| Center | Subjects Covered | Format | Monthly Price (Per Subject) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ustoz.uz** | Full Spectrum (Math, Bio, Hist, etc.) | Online (Video) & Offline | Offline: 500k – 800k UZS; Online: 39k – 150k |
| **Registan** | All DTM Subjects | Intensive Offline | 715k – 800k UZS |
| **PDP Junior** | Math, English, IT | Hybrid / Bootcamp | 900k – 1.5M UZS |
| **Real Science** | Math, Physics, Chemistry | Premium Offline | 1.2M – 1.5M UZS |
| **Repetitor-Servis** | Humanities focus | Traditional Offline | 510k – 550k UZS |

### Private Tutor Rates (Tashkent 2024-2025)
- **Standard Tutor:** 80,000 – 150,000 UZS per hour.
- **High-End (University Professors):** 200,000 – 500,000 UZS per hour.
- **Student Tutors:** 50,000 – 80,000 UZS per hour.
- **Full 5-Subject Prep Package:** 2,500,000 – 4,000,000 UZS per month.

## 4. Market Trends & Insights (2025)
1. **The "Milliy Sertifikat" Rush:** Students now take National Certificates in subjects like Mathematics or Biology during the school year to avoid the summer DTM exam.
2. **Hybrid Adoption:** Top centers (PDP, Ustoz) are moving to models with 2 days offline + digital LMS support.
3. **PISA Gap:** Existing platforms lack alignment with PISA metrics (Reading/Math), which **Sigma_Edu_3000** aims to fill via its 7-Step Journey.
