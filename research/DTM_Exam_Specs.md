# Uzbekistan DTM (BBA) Exam Specifications (2024-2025)

## 1. General Exam Structure
The Uzbekistan university entrance exam, administered by the **Bilimni Baholash Agentligi (BBA)** (formerly DTM), consists of a unified test with 5 subjects total.

| Block | Subjects | Questions | Points per Question | Max Points |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mandatory** | Native Language, Math, History of Uzbekistan | 10 + 10 + 10 = 30 | 1.1 | 33.0 |
| **Specialty 1** | Primary Major Subject | 30 | 3.1 | 93.0 |
| **Specialty 2** | Secondary Major Subject | 30 | 2.1 | 63.0 |
| **Total** | **5 Subjects** | **90 Questions** | | **189.0** |

- **Time Limit:** 180 minutes (3 hours).
- **Format:** Multiple Choice (4 options: A, B, C, D).
- **Passing Scores:** 
  - **State Grant (Scholarship):** Min 68.0 points.
  - **Tuition-Based (Contract):** Min 56.7 points.
- **Certificate Exemptions:** Applicants with B2 level (IELTS 5.5+, CEFR B2) get max 63.0 points for Foreign Language automatically.

## 3. Topic Breakdowns (Approximate Percentages)

### Mathematics (Matematika)
- **Algebra (65%):** Natural numbers (5%), Equations/Inequalities (20%), Functions (15%), Sequences (10%), Calculus (15%).
- **Geometry (35%):** Planimetry (20%), Stereometry (10%), Vectors (5%).

### Physics (Fizika)
- **Mechanics (30%):** Kinematics, Dynamics, Statics.
- **Molecular Physics & Thermo (20%):** Gas laws, Heat engines.
- **Electrodynamics & Optics (40%):** DC circuits, Magnetism, Geometric/Wave optics.
- **Quantum & Nuclear (10%):** Photoelectric effect, Radioactivity.

### Biology (Biologiya)
- **General Biology (35%):** Cytology, Genetics, Evolution, Ecology.
- **Human Anatomy (25%):** Organ systems, Physiology.
- **Zoology (20%):** Invertebrates and Vertebrates.
- **Botany (20%):** Plant anatomy and classification.

### Chemistry (Kimyo)
- **General Chemistry (35%):** Atomic structure, Periodic law, Bonding, Solutions.
- **Inorganic Chemistry (30%):** Metals, Non-metals, Compounds.
- **Organic Chemistry (35%):** Hydrocarbons, Alcohols, Aldehydes, Nitrogen compounds.

### History (Tarix)
- **History of Uzbekistan (60%):** Ancient states, Temurids, Khanates, Independence era.
- **World History (40%):** Ancient civilizations, Middle Ages, Modern history.

## 4. DTM Difficulty vs. School Curriculum
- **Alignment:** DTM questions are strictly based on the National School Textbooks (Grades 5-11).
- **Depth:** DTM questions often require higher-level application (e.g., multi-step physics problems or deep historical dates/names) compared to standard classroom tests.
- **Evolution:** Since 2021, there is a shift toward "competency-based" questions that require logical reasoning rather than pure memorization, though the majority still relies on factual recall.

## 5. Sample Specimen Questions (3-5 per subject)

### Mathematics
1. **Algebra:** Solve $\sqrt{x^2 - 5x + 6} = x - 2$. (Ans: $x=2$)
2. **Geometry:** Find the area of a triangle with sides 13, 14, 15. (Ans: 84)
3. **Calculus:** Find the derivative of $f(x) = \sin^2(3x)$. (Ans: $3\sin(6x)$)

### Physics
1. **Mechanics:** A body moves with $a=2m/s^2$ and $v_0=5m/s$. Distance covered in 4s? (Ans: 36m)
2. **Electricity:** Find the total resistance of two $10 \Omega$ resistors in parallel. (Ans: $5 \Omega$)
3. **Optics:** Find the focal length of a lens with $+2.0$ diopters. (Ans: 0.5m)

### Biology
1. **Cytology:** Which organelle synthesizes ATP? (Ans: Mitochondria)
2. **Genetics:** Crossing Aa x Aa results in what genotype ratio? (Ans: 1:2:1)
3. **Anatomy:** Which valve is between the left atrium and left ventricle? (Ans: Mitral/Bicuspid)

### Chemistry
1. **General:** Volume of $0.2M$ NaOH to neutralize $100ml$ of $0.1M$ $H_2SO_4$? (Ans: 100ml)
2. **Inorganic:** What is the oxidation state of Cr in $K_2Cr_2O_7$? (Ans: +6)
3. **Organic:** Product of propane bromination in light? (Ans: 2-bromopropane)

### Native Language (Uzbek)
1. **Grammar:** "Kitobni o'qidim" gapida qaysi kelishik qo'llangan? (Ans: Tushum kelishigi)
2. **Phonetics:** "Vatandosh" so'zida nechta jarangli undosh bor? (Ans: 4 ta: v, n, d, sh - wait, sh is jarangsiz. Correct: v, n, d)

### English
1. **Grammar:** If I ___ enough money, I would buy a new car. (Ans: had)
2. **Vocabulary:** Choose the synonym of "Enormous". (Ans: Huge)
