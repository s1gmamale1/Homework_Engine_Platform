## 2. 🇺🇿 Domestic Regulatory Requirements (Uzbekistan)

To operate as a national-scale LMS, NETS must meet the following four pillars of Uzbekistan's regulatory framework:

### 2.1 MPSB 'Darslik' Certification
*   **Requirement:** All digital content must undergo **Expert Examination (Ekspertiza)** by the Republican Scientific and Methodological Center.
*   **Metric:** 1:1 mapping to the **Milliy O‘quv Dasturi** (National Curriculum).
*   **Implementation:** Our Phase-by-Phase tagging (`textbook_ref`, `standard_ref`) is designed to automate this compliance report.

### 2.2 UzBMB (DTM) Official Partnership
*   **Requirement:** A **Cooperation Agreement (Hamkorlik shartnomasi)** is mandatory to use the "Official DTM" label.
*   **Benefit:** Provides legal access to the **Official Diagnostic Test Database**.
*   **Constraint:** Requires a revenue-share or licensing fee with the Agency.

### 2.3 IT Park Residency
*   **Requirement:** Register as an Uzbekistan LLC with "Software Development" or "IT Education" activity.
*   **Compliance:** 1% revenue contribution to the fund.
*   **Benefit:** 0% Corporate Tax, 0% VAT, and customs exemptions for server hardware.

### 2.4 Data Sovereignty (ZRU-547)
*   **Requirement:** **Article 27-1** mandates that personal data of Uzbek citizens (Name, Face ID, Grades) be stored on **physically local servers**.
*   **Action:** Migration from global clouds to Tashkent-based data centers (**UZINFOCOM** or **Uzcloud**) is a non-negotiable launch requirement.

## 3. 🌍 Global Quality & Accreditation Benchmarks

To compete internationally and attract premium subscribers, NETS targets these "Gold Standard" certifications:

### 3.1 ISTE Seal (Pedagogical Excellence)
*   **Focus:** Student-led learning and teacher empowerment.
*   **Requirement:** Dashboards must visualize **knowledge gaps** and provide **actionable insights**, not just raw scores.
*   **Alignment:** Our **Teacher Dashboard** (v1 spec) and **Tier 3 Socratic Tutoring** are designed for the ISTE Seal.

### 3.2 Quality Matters (QM) K-12 Rubric
*   **Focus:** **Alignment**.
*   **Requirement:** Instructional materials, learning activities, and assessments (PISA tasks) must be perfectly synchronized.
*   **Metric:** Every task must specify its `transition_skill` and PISA level (Layer 0 mandate).

### 3.3 ISO/IEC 40180 (E-Learning Quality)
*   **Focus:** **Lifecycle Quality**.
*   **Requirement:** A 7-phase quality management process (Needs → Analysis → Design → Dev → Implementation → Evaluation → Optimization).
*   **Alignment:** NETS's **7-Phase Engine** mirrors this lifecycle within every session.

### 3.4 PISA Alignment Verification (OECD/EAF)
*   **Accreditor:** bodies like **Education Alliance Finland (EAF)**.
*   **Focus:** "Transversal Competencies" (Critical Thinking, Creative Thinking).
*   **Requirement:** Content must force **active construction of knowledge** (PISA Level 5-6), not just rote recall.

## 4. ⚖️ Legal & Privacy (GDPR-K / COPPA / AADC)

As a K-11 platform, "High Privacy by Default" is the international standard:
*   **Principle:** **"Best Interests of the Child"** (UK AADC).
*   **Requirement:** No geolocation, no behavioral profiling for ads, and **age-appropriate privacy notices** (using icons/videos for G5-6).
*   **Action:** NETS must implement a **Child-First Privacy Policy** that exceeds basic Uzbek legal requirements to meet global EdTech standards.
