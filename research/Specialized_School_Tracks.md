# Specialized School Tracks in Uzbekistan

This document outlines the specialized education paths in Uzbekistan for K-11 students, detailing the differences in curriculum, assessment, and opportunities.

## 1. Presidential Schools (Prezident Maktablari)
The flagship educational institutions in Uzbekistan, managed by **PIIMA**.

- **Curriculum:** **Cambridge International Curriculum** (IGCSE and A-Levels).
- **Language of Instruction:** English (for STEAM subjects), Uzbek/Russian (for national subjects).
- **Core Focus:** STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics).
- **Admission:** Highly selective (<1% acceptance), based on critical thinking and logic tests.
- **Outcomes:** Students earn two diplomas: An International A-Level Diploma (recognized worldwide) and a National Certificate.
- **Curriculum Difference:** 
  - **Inquiry-based learning:** Focus on "Why" and "How" rather than "What".
  - **Assessment:** Practical labs and project-based assessments are weighted heavily.

## 2. Specialized Schools (Ixtisoslashtirilgan Maktablar)
Also under PIIMA or specific ministries (e.g., Al-Khwarizmi for ICT, Abu Ali ibn Sino for Biology/Chemistry).

- **Focus:** In-depth study of specific subjects (e.g., Math, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, ICT).
- **Admission:** Subject-specific exams (e.g., Math + IQ for Al-Khwarizmi).
- **Curriculum:** Enhanced National Curriculum with additional hours and advanced topics in the specialized field.

## 3. Academic Lyceums (Akademik Litseylar)
Two-year programs (Grades 10-11) often affiliated with major Universities.

- **Tracks:**
  - **Aniq fanlar (Technical):** Math, Physics.
  - **Aniq fanlar (Economic):** Math, Foreign Language.
  - **Tabiiy fanlar:** Biology, Chemistry.
  - **Ijtimoiy-gumanitar:** History, Foreign Language.
- **Admission Exam (2024):**
  - **IQ Test:** 20 questions (1.1 pts each).
  - **Major Subject 1:** 20 questions (3.1 pts each).
  - **Major Subject 2:** 20 questions (3.1 pts each).
  - **Max Score:** 146 points.

## 4. Olympiad Tracks (Fan Olimpiadalari)
Uzbekistan has a robust 4-stage national Olympiad system.

- **Stages:**
  1. **School:** Universal participation.
  2. **District/City:** Top students from schools.
  3. **Regional (Viloyat):** Top students from districts.
  4. **National (Republic):** Top students from regions.
- **Nufuzli Olimpiadalar (Prestigious):** Math, Physics, Bio, Chem, Informatics. Winners of the National level get direct admission to Universities without exams.
- **International Participation:** IMO (Math), IPhO (Physics), IChO (Chemistry), IBO (Biology), IOI (Informatics).
- **Sample Questions (Olympiad Level):**
  - **Math:** $x^2 + y^2 = 20, x + y = 6 \implies xy = ?$ (Ans: 8)
  - **Physics:** Calculate average velocity for two halves of a trip with $v_1=40, v_2=60$. (Ans: 48 km/h)
  - **Informatics:** Dynamic programming or graph theory problems (e.g., "Shortest path in a weighted DAG").

## 5. IT Programs & Ecosystem
- **One Million Uzbek Coders (uzbekcoders.uz):**
  - Collaboration with Udacity.
  - Courses: Data Analysis, Android, Full-stack, Frontend.
  - Free access, certificates, and "Nanodegree" grants for top performers.
- **IT Park Uzbekistan:**
  - **IT-Academy:** Intensive vocational courses for 3-9 months.
  - **IT Park University:** First digital university in Uzbekistan focused on Software Engineering.
- **Robocontest.uz:** The main platform for competitive programming (Olympiad Informatics) in Uzbekistan.
