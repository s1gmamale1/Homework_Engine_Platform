# Subject-Specific Topic Progressions: Uzbekistan K-11 to Expert/Olympiad

This document outlines the natural difficulty progression from basic school curriculum (G5-11) to extended (Lyceum) and expert (Olympiad/University) levels for key subjects in Uzbekistan.

---

## 1. Mathematics (Matematika)
The progression shifts from functional arithmetic to abstract, proof-based reasoning.

| Level | Difficulty | Focus Topics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic (G5-11)** | Standard | Arithmetic, basic algebra (linear/quadratic), plane geometry, percentages, logarithms, basic trigonometry, and probability (G10/11). |
| **Extended (Lyceum)** | DTM L2/L3 | Advanced algebra, stereometry, derivatives, integrals, complex numbers, and parameters. Focus on solving standardized entrance exam patterns. |
| **Expert (Olympiad)** | IMO Standard | **Number Theory:** Modular arithmetic, Diophantine equations, primitive roots. **Combinatorics:** Graph theory, Pigeonhole principle, recurrence relations. **Geometry:** Advanced centers, radical axis, projective geometry. |

**DTM Styles:** 
- **Level 1 (Easy):** One-step calculations (e.g., linear equations).
- **Level 2 (Medium):** Application of theorems (e.g., progressions, logs).
- **Level 3 (Hard):** Multi-step analysis (e.g., integrals, stereometry).

---

## 2. Physics (Fizika)
Transitions from phenomenological understanding to mathematical modeling.

| Level | Difficulty | Focus Topics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic (G7-11)** | Conceptual | Mechanics (G7/10), Thermodynamics (G8/10), Electrodynamics, Optics, and Quantum basics (G11). Focus on literacy and basic formulas. |
| **Extended (Lyceum)** | Technical | Rigid body dynamics, entropy, internal energy, laser basics, and circuit analysis with multi-step formulas. |
| **Expert (Olympiad)** | IPhO Standard | Non-inertial frames, Lagrangian mechanics, statistical mechanics (Boltzmann), Schrödinger equation (qualitative), and advanced calculus applications. |

---

## 3. Chemistry (Kimyo)
Moves from descriptive chemistry to complex synthesis and physical laws.

| Level | Difficulty | Focus Topics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic (G7-11)** | Foundational | Periodic table, basic nomenclature, main group elements, simple stoichiometry, and organic basics (alkanes/alkenes). |
| **Extended (Lyceum)** | Specialized | Reaction mechanisms (Sn1/Sn2), chemical kinetics, electrochemistry (Nernst), d-block elements, and detailed biochemistry. |
| **Expert (Olympiad)** | IChO Standard | **Organic:** Multistep synthesis, spectroscopy (NMR/IR). **Physical:** Phase diagrams, enzymatic kinetics. **Inorganic:** Crystal field theory. |

---

## 4. Biology (Biologiya)
Shifts from taxonomy and anatomy to molecular genetics and biotechnology.

| Level | Difficulty | Focus Topics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic (G5-11)** | Descriptive | Botany (G5/6), Zoology (G7), Human Anatomy (G8/9), General Biology (G10/11) focusing on Mendelian laws and basic cytology. |
| **Extended (Lyceum)** | Analytical | Complex inheritance (epistasis), detailed Central Dogma, tissue culture, and industrial microbiology. |
| **Expert (Olympiad)** | IBO Standard | Population genetics (Hardy-Weinberg), bioinformatics, CRISPR-Cas9, PCR techniques, and advanced experimental design. |

---

## 5. English (Ingliz tili)
Progression aligned with CEFR levels and international certification.

| Level | Difficulty | CEFR / IELTS Target |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic (G5-11)** | Functional | **A1 to B1/B2**. Focus on school textbooks and basic communication. |
| **Extended (Lyceum)** | Academic | **B2+ to C1 (IELTS 6.5–7.5)**. Focus on academic reading, writing, and test-taking strategies. |
| **Expert (Olympiad)** | Proficiency | **C1+ to C2 (IELTS 8.0+)**. Focus on linguistics, complex academic synthesis, and creative writing. |

---

## 6. Uzbek Language & Literature (Ona tili va Adabiyot)
From prescriptive grammar to theoretical philology.

| Level | Difficulty | Focus Topics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic (G5-11)** | Prescriptive | Correct spelling (X/H), morphology (12 parts of speech), basic syntax, and literary works of Navoi/Babur. |
| **Extended (Lyceum)** | Stylistic | Advanced grammar parsing, scientific/artistic style distinctions, and preparation for high-speed DTM test patterns. |
| **Expert (Olympiad)** | Philological | Old Uzbek (Eski o'zbek tili), etymology, historical linguistics, and creative literary synthesis (essays). |

---

## 7. History (O'zbekiston/Jahon Tarixi)
From national narrative to scientific historiography.

| Level | Difficulty | Focus Topics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic (G5-11)** | Narrative | Patriotic linear history (Ancient to Modern). Focus on Independence, Timurids, and Jadeed movements. |
| **Extended (Lyceum)** | Source-based | Regional focus (e.g., Oriental studies), source criticism, and analysis of "The Great Game." |
| **Expert (Olympiad)** | Methodological | Comparative history, Annales School vs. Marxist vs. Liberal historiography, and post-colonial theory. |
