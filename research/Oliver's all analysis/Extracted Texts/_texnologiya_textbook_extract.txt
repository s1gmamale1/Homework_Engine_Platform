DOMAINS/SUBJECT AREAS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This is a MIXED-domain technology textbook covering TWO main tracks
(yo'nalishlar) that students study:

TRACK 1: TEXNOLOGIYA VA DIZAYN (Technology and Design)
  - Modern technology and machinery concepts
  - Woodworking (yog'ochga ishlov berish) - carpentry workshop skills
  - Materials science (wood, polymers, composites, metals)
  - Machine mechanisms and transmissions (belt, chain, gear, worm, friction, rack)
  - Technical modeling and construction (transport vehicles)
  - Energy production and utilization (mechanical, electrical, solar, nuclear)
  - Electric motors, batteries, hydraulic transmissions
  - 3D printing concepts (3D pen/3D ruchka)

TRACK 2: SERVIS XIZMATI (Service Industry)
  - Food processing technology (oziq-ovqat mahsulotlariga ishlov berish)
    - Cooking/culinary skills (pazandachilik)
    - Table setting (dasturxon tuzash)
    - Vegetable processing, salads
    - Egg dishes
    - Sandwiches, hot and cold drinks
    - National dishes (qaynatma sho'rva, mastava)
  - Textile and sewing (gazlamashunoslik / tikuvchilik)
    - Fabric science (cotton, flax fibers, weaving)
    - Pattern making (andaza)
    - Cutting and sewing (apron/fartuk, headscarf/ro'mol)
    - Ironing techniques
    - Embroidery (kashtachilik) - traditional Uzbek needlework

This is NOT purely digital tech. It is a practical life-skills technology course
covering woodworking, cooking, sewing, and basic engineering concepts - typical
of post-Soviet "Technology" (Технология) curriculum for middle school.

SYMBOL/ICON LEGEND (from page 4)

The textbook uses the following symbols to mark content types:

  - Muammoli topshiriq          = Problem-based assignment
  - Amaliy mashg'ulot           = Practical lesson (Technology & Design track)
  - Mustahkamlash uchun savollar = Questions for consolidation (Tech & Design)
  - Amaliy mashg'ulot           = Practical lesson (Service track)
  - Mustahkamlash uchun savollar = Questions for consolidation (Service track)
  - Jihozlar                    = Equipment/tools
  - Loyiha ishi                 = Project work
  - Diqqat qiling!              = Attention!/Warning!
  - Ishni bajarish tartibi      = Procedure for completing the work
  - QR Code                     = Digital supplementary content via QR/web

================================================================================
PEDAGOGICAL FEATURES OBSERVED
================================================================================
1. Each section ends with "Mustahkamlash uchun savollar" (Consolidation Questions)
   - typically 4 review questions per section
2. Practical lessons (Amaliy mashg'ulot) are numbered sequentially within each
   sub-topic and include: Jihozlar (equipment), Ishni bajarish tartibi (procedure)
3. Project work (Loyiha ishi) sections for independent student projects
4. QR codes linking to digital supplementary content
5. Two-track system: students study either Technology & Design OR Service track
6. Cross-curricular: combines engineering concepts, life skills, and traditional crafts
