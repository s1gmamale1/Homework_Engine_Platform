# Uzbekistan Lyceum Detailed Curricula: Phase 2 Research

This document outlines the detailed curriculum topic lists for Uzbekistan's **Akademik litseylar** (Academic Lyceums) and **Fizika-matematika litseylari** (Physics-Mathematics Lyceums), with a focus on "Delta" topics—those taught beyond the standard Grade 10-11 school curriculum.

---

## 1. Overview of Lyceum Curriculum (2024-2025)
Starting from the 2024-2025 academic year, the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation has implemented a **Unification Strategy**. Lyceum curricula are now aligned with the first-year programs of partner Universities.

- **System:** Semester-based (2 semesters per year).
- **Assessment:** Credit-module system (Kredit-modul tizimi), allowing for credit recognition when entering University.
- **Focus:** High-intensity specialized tracks with significantly more hours allocated to "major" subjects compared to standard schools.

---

## 2. Track-Specific Topic Lists

### A. Engineering / Math-Physics Track (Aniq fanlar)
*Focus: Deepening mathematical abstraction and physical modeling.*

| Subject | Core Topics (Standard K-11) | **Delta Topics (Lyceum Only)** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mathematics** | Linear/Quadratic equations, basic logs, trigonometry, basic probability. | **Set Theory & Logic:** Formal proofs, boolean logic. **Complex Numbers:** Operations, trigonometric form. **Calculus:** Limits, advanced derivatives, definite/indefinite integrals. **Stereometry:** Advanced 3D geometry of complex polyhedra. |
| **Physics** | Basic kinematics, Newton's laws, thermal expansion, basic circuits. | **Rigid Body Dynamics:** Moments of inertia, torque. **Fluid Mechanics:** Bernoulli's principle, viscosity. **Electrodynamics:** Kirchhoff’s Laws for complex circuits, alternating current. **Quantum/Nuclear:** Bohr’s atom, radioactive decay constants, laser physics. |

### B. Medical / Bio-Chem Track (Tabiiy fanlar)
*Focus: Molecular biology, organic mechanisms, and chemical kinetics.*

| Subject | Core Topics (Standard K-11) | **Delta Topics (Lyceum Only)** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Biology** | General botany/zoology, Mendelian genetics, human anatomy. | **Cytology:** Deep dive into ATP synthesis, Krebs cycle, Photosynthesis (Light/Dark phases). **Genetics:** Population genetics (Hardy-Weinberg), linked inheritance, epistasis. **Biotechnology:** PCR, cloning basics, industrial microbiology. |
| **Chemistry** | Periodic table, basic stoichiometry, alkanes/alkenes basics. | **Physical Chemistry:** Chemical kinetics (reaction rates), thermodynamics (Entropy, Enthalpy, Gibbs free energy). **Organic Mechanisms:** Sn1, Sn2, E1, E2 reaction types. **Inorganic:** Detailed study of d-block elements (complex compounds). |

### C. Humanities / Law Track (Ijtimoiy-gumanitar)
*Focus: Legal theory, historical synthesis, and philology.*

| Subject | Core Topics (Standard K-11) | **Delta Topics (Lyceum Only)** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **History** | Narrative timeline of Uzbekistan and World history. | **Historiography:** Source criticism, analysis of historical documents. **Contemporary History:** Deep study of the Cold War, Globalization, and the "Yangi O'zbekiston" Development Strategy. |
| **Law** | Basic rights and duties of citizens. | **Jurisprudence:** Theory of State and Law. **Constitutional Law:** Detailed analysis of the 2023 Constitution. **Specialized Law:** Civil, Family, Labor, and Criminal law. **Criminology (New):** Basics of crime prevention (introduced 2024). |

---

### D. Foreign Philology (Xorijiy filologiya)
*Focus: Reaching C1 level proficiency and academic writing.*
- **English:** Shift from functional grammar to **Academic English (EAP)**, focusing on IELTS/CEFR C1 preparation.
- **Delta:** Linguistics, translation theory, and literature analysis in the target language.

---

## 3. The "Delta" Gap Analysis
The "Delta" represents the competitive advantage of Lyceum students. 

1.  **Mathematical Rigor:** Inclusion of **Complex Numbers** and **Calculus** (Limits/Integrals) which are often only touched upon or entirely absent in the standard "Mainstream" curriculum.
2.  **Chemical Synthesis:** Focus on **Reaction Mechanisms** (why atoms move) rather than just memorizing products.
3.  **Physical Law:** Introduction of **Kirchhoff's Laws** and **Rigid Body Dynamics**, providing a foundation for Engineering degrees.
4.  **Legal Depth:** Preparation for the **Law Entrance Exam** (TDYU) requires knowledge of specific articles of the Criminal/Civil codes not found in school textbooks.

## 4. Official Resources & Documents
- **Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation (edu.uz):** The primary authority for Lyceum "Namunaviy o'quv rejalari" (Model Curricula).
- **UzBMB (Formerly DTM):** Provides the official test specifications (Fan dasturlari) which define the topics eligible for entrance exams.
- **Partner University Portals:** (e.g., tsul.uz for Law Lyceums, ttpuj.uz for Turin Poly Lyceum) contain specific syllabi used for track-specific subjects.
