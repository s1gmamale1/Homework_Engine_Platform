# International Language Certification Specifications

This document outlines the structure, mapping, and requirements for international language certifications relevant to the Uzbekistan K-11 education system and prep market.

## 1. IELTS Test Structure & Specifications

| Section | Time Limit | Questions/Tasks | Task Types |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Listening** | 30 mins (+10 transfer) | 40 questions | Multiple choice, matching, map/diagram labeling, form/note completion. |
| **Reading** | 60 mins | 40 questions | T/F/NG, matching headings, sentence completion, multiple choice. |
| **Writing** | 60 mins | 2 tasks | **Task 1:** Report (Academic) or Letter (General). **Task 2:** Essay (250+ words). |
| **Speaking** | 11–14 mins | 3 parts | **P1:** Intro/Interview. **P2:** Long turn (Cue card). **P3:** Discussion. |

### Scoring Criteria (Writing & Speaking)
Both sections are graded on four equally weighted criteria (25% each):
- **Writing:** Task Achievement/Response, Coherence & Cohesion, Lexical Resource, Grammatical Range & Accuracy.
- **Speaking:** Fluency & Coherence, Lexical Resource, Grammatical Range & Accuracy, Pronunciation.

### Band 6 vs. Band 7 Comparison
| Feature | Band 6 (Competent User) | Band 7 (Good User) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Writing** | Addresses all parts; mix of simple/complex sentences. | Addresses all parts clearly; variety of complex structures. |
| **Speaking** | Willing to speak at length; some repetition/hesitation. | Speaks at length without noticeable effort; idiomatic usage. |
| **Vocabulary** | Generally adequate; some errors in choice/spelling. | Uses less common items with awareness of style. |
| **Grammar** | Mix of simple/complex; errors rarely impede meaning. | Wide range of structures; many error-free sentences. |

## 2. CEFR to IELTS Mapping Table

| CEFR Level | IELTS Band | CEFR Global Descriptor | IELTS Official Descriptor |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **C2 (Mastery)** | 8.5 – 9.0 | Can understand with ease virtually everything heard or read. | **Expert User:** Fully operational command; accurate and fluent. |
| **C1 (Advanced)** | 7.0 – 8.0 | Can understand demanding, longer texts; expresses ideas fluently. | **Good / Very Good User:** Operational command; handles complex language. |
| **B2 (Upper Int.)** | 5.5 – 6.5 | Can understand main ideas of complex text; interact without strain. | **Competent User:** Generally effective command despite inaccuracies. |
| **B1 (Intermediate)** | 4.0 – 5.0 | Can understand main points on familiar matters; simple connected text. | **Modest User:** Partial command; copes with overall meaning. |
| **A2 (Elementary)** | 3.0 – 3.5 | Can understand sentences and frequently used expressions. | **Extremely Limited User:** Understands only general meaning. |
| **A1 (Beginner)** | 2.0 – 2.5 | Can use familiar everyday expressions and very basic phrases. | **Intermittent User:** No real communication possible. |

## 3. Uzbekistan IELTS Requirements (2024–2025)

### University Admissions
- **Master’s/PhD:** Mandatory certificate. Foreign Language (C1), Humanities/Business/ICT (B2), Science/Engineering/Health (B1).
- **Undergraduate:** B2 (IELTS 5.5+) grants maximum points (51.0) for the foreign language block in state exams.
- **International Universities:** Westminster (5.5-6.0), New Uzbekistan University (5.5-6.0), Webster (6.0).

### Employment & Incentives
- **Teachers:** B2 (IELTS 5.5+) is mandatory for foreign language teachers in state schools.
- **Bonuses:** C1 (IELTS 7.0+) grants a **50% monthly salary bonus** for teachers.
- **Youth Rewards:** Full exam fee reimbursement for C1 (7.0+) and one-time cash reward (~1M UZS).

## 4. Other Popular Certifications in Uzbekistan

| Exam | Target | Key Feature | Validity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **TOEFL iBT** | US/STEM | New Adaptive Format (shorter, real-time difficulty adjustment). | 2 Years |
| **Cambridge (FCE/CAE)** | Teachers/Long-term | Permanent qualification; CAE (C1) is highly valued for teachers. | Lifetime |
| **TORFL (TRKI)** | Russian Universities | 5 parts (Grammar, Read, Write, Listen, Speak). B2 for Master's. | 2 Years |
| **National (CEFR)** | Local Admission | Cheaper alternative provided by Bilimni Baholash Agentligi (BBA). | 2 Years |
